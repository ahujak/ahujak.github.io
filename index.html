About

I am a postdoctoral fellow at Mila-Quebec AI Institute. Before coming to Mila, I was an AI Resident at IBM Research, Thomas J. Watson Research Center, Yorktown Heights, NY. I completed my PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering from University of California, Los Angeles and B.tech-M.tech Dual Degree in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur.

I am broadly interested in problems in the areas of machine learning, causality, and optimization. In the past, I worked on optimization methods for resource allocation problems. In recent years, I have worked on the theory and methods for out-of-distribution generalization in machine learning.
